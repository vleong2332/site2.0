The Context

The VP of Information Technology of Wycliffe Associates needed a simple website about our team that can also act as a job application portal. He must also be able to edit the content without having to involve me.

The Stack

This is a Wordpress site built with a third-party brand of Beaver Builder.

My Role

For this project, I acted more as a designer and content co-creator than a developer since it's Wordpress and is hosted by a third-party.

The Process

I was given admin access to a blank Wordpress site and was let loose. The only restriction was time (less than 1 working week). I utilized the pre-made components provided by the page builder to create the layouts and contents. I did have to write a few custom CSS rules to correct the responsiveness.
